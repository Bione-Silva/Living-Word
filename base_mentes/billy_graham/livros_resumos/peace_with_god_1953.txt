TITLE: Peace with God (1953)
AUTHOR: Billy Graham
PAGES EQUIVALENT: 250 pages

CORE THEMES:
The problem of empty humanity vs eternal peace.
Sin is defined as the disease causing all societal and personal anxiety.
The cross is the only bridge.

CHAPTER SUMMARIES & CONCEPTS:
1. The Great Quest: Humanity is on a tireless search for peace—peace in the world, peace in the home, and peace in the heart. But true peace cannot be found in wealth, power, or education.
2. The Biblical Fact of Sin: Every manifestation of evil is the result of basic sin. Sin is a disease of the human heart that separates man from God.
3. The Only Remedy: God proved His love on the Cross. Jesus Christ took the penalty of human sin.
4. The Process of Conversion: Acknowledgment, Repentance, and Faith. It is not an intellectual decision only; it is a total surrender of the will.
5. The Threefold Peace: 
   - Peace WITH God (Justification)
   - Peace OF God (Sanctification and internal serenity)
   - Future Peace (Glorification)
